Where we source our vegetables from

We mainly source our vegetables from 3 locations:

  • Sunrise Farms, Matale: Experience sustainable agriculture at its finest with Sunrise Farms in Matale. Our partner farm cultivates a diverse range of organic vegetables using traditional methods, ensuring freshness and quality in every harvest.
  • Highland Gardens, Nuwara Eliya: Discover premium high-altitude vegetables from Highland Gardens in Nuwara Eliya. Nestled amidst mist-covered hills, our farm specializes in specialty crops known for their unique flavors and textures.
  • Coastal Greenfields, Hambantota: Taste the freshness of tropical vegetables from Coastal Greenfields in Hambantota. Our farm harnesses abundant sunshine and rich coastal soils to produce flavorful vegetables grown sustainably with respect for the environment.
